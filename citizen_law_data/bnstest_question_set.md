# BNStest Evaluation Question Set

Use this set to benchmark retrieval + grounding after each ingestion run.

## Format
- Ask exactly as written.
- Expected answer should be concise and citation-grounded.
- If evidence is missing in retrieved chunks, expected behavior is: "insufficient evidence".

## A. Act Basics

1. Q: What is the short title of this Act?
   Expected: The short title is "Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023".

2. Q: How does this Act come into force?
   Expected: It comes into force on a date appointed by the Central Government by notification in the Official Gazette, with different dates possible for different provisions.

3. Q: Does the Act apply to offences committed outside India in some cases?
   Expected: Yes, it applies in specified cases, including Indian citizens abroad and offences targeting computer resources in India.

4. Q: In the Act, does the word "offence" include certain acts committed outside India?
   Expected: Yes, the explanation states that such acts are included if punishable when committed in India.

## B. Definitions (Section 2)

5. Q: What does "child" mean under this Act?
   Expected: A child means any person below eighteen years of age.

6. Q: What does "document" include under this Act?
   Expected: It includes matter expressed by letters/figures/marks and includes electronic and digital records intended or usable as evidence.

7. Q: How is "gender" clarified in the definitions?
   Expected: "He" and its derivatives are used for male, female, or transgender persons.

8. Q: What is the definition of "good faith"?
   Expected: Nothing is done or believed in good faith if done or believed without due care and attention.

9. Q: What is "injury" under this Act?
   Expected: Any harm illegally caused to a person in body, mind, reputation, or property.

10. Q: What does "person" include?
    Expected: It includes any company, association, or body of persons, whether incorporated or not.

11. Q: What is "wrongful gain"?
    Expected: Gain by unlawful means of property to which the person gaining is not legally entitled.

12. Q: What is "wrongful loss"?
    Expected: Loss by unlawful means of property to which the person losing is legally entitled.

## C. General Explanations (Section 3)

13. Q: Are offence definitions in this Act to be read subject to General Exceptions?
    Expected: Yes, definitions and penal provisions are subject to General Exceptions even if not repeated.

14. Q: If property is with a person's clerk or servant on that person's account, whose possession is it?
    Expected: It is considered in that person's possession.

15. Q: When several persons act in furtherance of common intention, how is liability treated?
    Expected: Each person is liable as if the act were done by that person alone.
